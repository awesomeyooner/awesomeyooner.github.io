New Technology: AI

Artificial Intelligence has started to rise up in it's popularity, mainly from ChatBots like ChatGPT. There are many different ways the idea of AI is being used, the mostly known one is for ChatBots. Other ways include, learning to play a game, being able to recognize letters and numbers, autonomous driving, vision pipelines, and many more.
